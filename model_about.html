A Decision Tree is a simple and powerful prediction method. It works like a game of 20 Questions, whittling down possibilities through successive questions to get to a final answer. The model:

  • outputs the likelihood of an outcome (0-100%)
  • presents an explicit logic of deduction
  • handles categorical predictors (e.g. hot/cold) as easily as continuous predictors (e.g. temperature in degrees Fahrenheit)
